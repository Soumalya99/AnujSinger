About Anuj Sharma

Born in Rehan, Himachal Pradesh, Anuj Sharma’s musical journey began early, deeply inspired by his elder brother, a scientist and his greatest supporter. What started as a childhood passion took shape during his college years, where stage performances revealed music as his true calling.

Anuj rose to national recognition as the runner-up of Indian Idol Season 2 (2006), a pivotal moment that launched his career as a professional singer. Since then, he has continued to build his legacy, collaborating for over a decade with acclaimed music director Hitesh (Swar Mystry). Together, they’ve crafted melodies that are rich in emotion, depth, and originality.

Anuj believes music is more than sound — it’s a story, a connection, and a feeling. His soulful voice and heartfelt performances continue to touch lives and resonate with audiences across generations.
